find duplicate records

```php
DB::table('users')
  ->select('id', 'email', DB::raw('COUNT(email)'))
  ->whereNull('deleted_at')
  ->havingRaw('COUNT(email) > 1')
  ->groupBy('email')
  ->get()
  ->toArray();
```
